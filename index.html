03 · About

ABOUT
ME

I'm Clason Peter, a data analyst who believes the most important part of any analysis is what happens after the numbers. I care about making sure the right people can understand the data and actually do something with it.

My background in marketing gives me a perspective that most analysts don't have. I learned early that there are always human needs behind the numbers: someone trying to make a decision, solve a problem, or justify a direction. That understanding shapes how I frame every analysis. I don't just ask what the data says; I ask what the audience needs to hear, and how to make the answer land.

Data storytelling is at the center of everything I do. I build the narrative around findings so that decisions aren't just informed, they're obvious. Numbers without context are just noise; I turn them into a point of view.

I'm expanding my toolkit into data science and engineering, building the systems and models that make insight scalable, not just presentable.
