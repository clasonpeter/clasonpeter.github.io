The Problem - ED Analytics

One patient segment was quietly overwhelming an entire Emergency Department.

Across 50,000 patient visits, the ED appeared to be functioning, but the numbers told a different story. Patients were leaving without being seen at nearly four times the industry benchmark. Boarding rates were dangerously high. The question wasn't just "what is happening", it was "why, and where is it coming from?"

7.6% LWBS Rate

Nearly 4x the 2% industry benchmark. Patients leaving without being seen is both a safety risk and a revenue loss.

19.7% Boarding Rate

Over 1 in 5 patients were boarded, blocking bed turnover and creating a bottleneck that cascades across the entire department.

ESI 3 The Driver

Mid-acuity patients flagged as ESI 3 were identified as the primary source of inefficiency, the segment where intervention would have the greatest impact.

So What

The data points to a clear intervention.

Targeted triage protocol improvements for ESI 3 patients; faster initial assessment, better fast-track routing, and proactive bed management, could meaningfully reduce both the LWBS rate and boarding backlog. This analysis doesn't just describe a problem. It identifies where to act and what to expect if you do.
